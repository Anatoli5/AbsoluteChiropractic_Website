Common Questions

Everything you need to know about your visit.

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross, Aetna, Cigna, and Medicare. We also offer flexible payment plans for patients without coverage.

Your first visit includes a comprehensive consultation, physical examination, and if necessary, digital X-rays. We'll discuss your health history and goals to create a personalized care plan.

Most patients experience immediate relief. Adjustments are generally painless, though you might feel some mild soreness afterwards, similar to a workout, as your body adapts to better alignment.

Initial consultations typically take 45-60 minutes. Follow-up adjustments are efficient and usually take 15-20 minutes, respecting your busy schedule.
